Getting Started with ASM Platform

Welcome to the ASM (Attack Surface Management) platform! This guide will help you get started with our tool and begin securing your digital assets.

What is Attack Surface Management?

Attack Surface Management is the process of discovering, cataloging, and monitoring all of your organization's external-facing digital assets.

First Steps

  1. Set up your account: Complete your profile information.
  2. Add a domain: Start by scanning a domain you own.
  3. Run vulnerability scans: Check for security issues.
  4. Review your dashboard: See an overview of vulnerabilities.

Subdomain Discovery

Subdomain discovery is the first step in understanding your attack surface. This process identifies all subdomains associated with your main domain.

How to Perform a Subdomain Scan

  1. Navigate to the Home page.
  2. Enter the root domain you want to scan.
  3. Click the "Scan Now" button.
  4. Review the discovered subdomains.

Vulnerability Scanning

After discovering your subdomains, the next step is to scan them for security vulnerabilities.

How to Run a Vulnerability Scan

  1. Go to the "Scan" page from the menu.
  2. Select a domain and subdomain.
  3. Choose a vulnerability category.
  4. Click "Start Scan".

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical scan take?

Scan duration depends on several factors:

  • Subdomain discovery: 5-15 minutes for most domains
  • Single vulnerability scan: 2-5 minutes per subdomain
Are the scans intrusive?

Our scans are designed to be non-intrusive and safe. We do not perform any exploits that could damage your systems.
