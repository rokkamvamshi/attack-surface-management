Welcome to ASM Platform!

Let's get you set up to discover and secure your attack surface.

This quick tour will help you understand how our platform works and how to get the most out of it. Here's what you'll learn:

  • How to discover your subdomains
  • How to scan for security vulnerabilities
  • How to interpret scan results
  • How to set up notifications and monitoring

Discover Your Attack Surface

First, you'll need to identify your digital assets.

The first step in securing your organization is understanding what you need to protect:

How to perform a subdomain scan:
  1. Navigate to the Home page
  2. Enter your root domain (e.g., example.com)
  3. Click "Scan Now"
  4. Wait for the scan to complete - this may take a few minutes
  5. Review the discovered subdomains

The platform will find all subdomains associated with your main domain, giving you a complete picture of your digital footprint.

Scan for Vulnerabilities

Find security issues across your attack surface.

After discovering your subdomains, it's time to scan them for security vulnerabilities:

How to perform a vulnerability scan:
  1. Go to the Scan page
  2. Select a domain from the dropdown
  3. Select a subdomain to scan
  4. Choose which vulnerabilities to check for
  5. Click "Start Scan"
  6. View results in your dashboard

Vulnerability scans can identify issues like XSS, SQL injection, misconfigurations, and more. They're essential for maintaining your security posture.

Understand Your Results

Interpret findings and take action.

Dashboard Features:
